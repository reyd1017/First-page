About Me

Thank you for visiting my page, My Name is Rey Diaz and I have been doing photography for 4 years. I started by helping a buddy of mine take video and photos at weddings. Everything took off from there, I fell in love with everything that photography had to offer. I have captured and seen some very beautiful moments "kodak moments". That's what I strive to do, capture all those special moments when life happens anytime of day. My thought is, life is beautiful there are special moments in any ocasion, one just needs to be patient and ready to capture those moments.
